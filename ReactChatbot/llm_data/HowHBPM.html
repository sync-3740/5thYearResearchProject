How to take home blood pressure measurement readings?

(1 Reviews)

Number and time of readings

  • Early morning and evening readings
  • Three readings ( taken at  1 minute interval)

Before you take your reading

  1. It is important to understand that there are many factors that can cause your blood pressure to rise for a short period of time
  2. It is advised that you should not smoke, have a beverage which contains caffeine for 30 minutes before your take your blood pressure reading.
  3. You should also avoid measuring your blood pressure when you need to use the toilet
  4. When measuring your blood pressure, it is important to use the same arm. This should be the arm that has been used to measure blood pressure by your doctor or nurse
  5. Do not wear any tight or restrictive clothing around the arm that you are measuring your blood pressure. During this time, you should be sitting down in a quiet place, try to relax and avoid speaking to anyone

Taking your reading

  1. Place the cuff on your arm, and follow the instructions that come with your monitor. The bottom of the cuff should be approximately 2cm above the bend in your elbow
  2. When you are measuring your blood pressure, make sure you are sitting down when taking your blood pressure readings and that you have both your fee flat on the floor. Please do not cross your legs as this can raise your blood pressure
  3. The arm that you are using to measure your blood pressure should be well supported on affirm surface such as a table or desk, with your palm facing up and this should be at the same level as your heart
  4. Take your reading following the instructions that came with your monitor. When your blood pressure readings are being taken, you should not talk and try to relax
  5. You should take am minimum of two readings with a minute intervals in between
  6. After each measurement, you should write the reading down in your blood pressure dairy.
  7. Remember to write down the each numbers that appear on the screen, do not round the numbers up or down
  8. If the first two readings are very different, take 2 or 3 further readings.
  9. You should write all of your readings down in your blood pressure dairy.
